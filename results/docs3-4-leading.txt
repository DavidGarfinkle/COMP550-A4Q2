5 Years Of Incorrect Claims & Forecasts About Tesla From TSLA Bear Mark Spiegel

November 4th, 2018 by Zachary Shahan

There have been a handful of particularly loud Tesla bears or critics over the past several years.. One of those people, Andrew Left of Citron Research, recently made a U-turn in his position on Tesla (citing 4 CleanTechnica charts of Tesla sales along the way).. Another one is Jim Chanos, who we’ll probably address again before long, and another is Mark Spiegel.. Like Chanos, Spiegel gets put on major TV shows rather frequently for his “expertise” on Tesla and is routinely quoted in major financial news outlets.
